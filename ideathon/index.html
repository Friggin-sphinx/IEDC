Internal Hackathon Guidelines

  • All team members should be SJCET students; inter-college teams are not allowed. Include students from different branches within the college.
  • Each team should consist of 6 members, including one designated team leader.
  • Ensure that each team includes at least one female member to promote gender diversity.
  • The first phase of the Internal Hackathon should be an Ideathon. Only the selected teams from the Ideathon will be eligible to participate in the Internal Hackathon.
  • The participants can select the problem statements from the Smart India Hackathon website ( https://www.sih.gov.in/ )
  • For the software edition of the hackathon (digital product development), the majority of team members must possess strong programming skills
  • For the hardware edition, encourage multi-disciplinary teams with a mix of Mechanical Engineers, Electronic Engineers, Product Designers, Programmers, etc., to foster a holistic approach to problem-solving and product development.
  • The winners will be eligible for participating in Smart India Hackathon.
